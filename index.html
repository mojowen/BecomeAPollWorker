Frequently Asked Questions

What Will I Do As a Poll Worker?

Your responsibilities will vary depending on what state you live in, but generally poll workers:

  1. Prepare the polling place for voting.
  2. Set up the voting equipment.
  3. Process the voters.
  4. Demonstrate voting procedures to the voters.
  5. Close the polling place.
  6. Canvass and report the results.
  7. Assist voter if requested by the voter.

What Is Required?

The requirements will vary depending on what state you live in, but generally poll workers must:

  • Be at least 18 years of age and a registered voter in the state.
  • Read and write English.
  • Work on Election Day while the polls are open (hours vary).
  • Attend a mandatory training session (approx. 2-3 hours).
  • Have transportation to the polling place.
